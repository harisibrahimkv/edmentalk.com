4. Professional Fees and Payment Terms

4.1 Service Fees

The Total Professional Fees for the Engagement Period, covering strategy development, planning, coordination, and campaign management services across all six pillars as outlined in Section 2, are defined in the Financials section of this Agreement.

4.2 Payment Schedule

50% of the Total Professional Fees is due upon execution of this Agreement.
The remaining 50% is due prior to commencement of the Engagement Period.
Work shall commence only upon receipt of the initial 50% payment.

4.3 Exclusions from Service Fees

The stated professional fees cover only the Service Provider's coordination and management services. The following costs are expressly excluded and shall be borne directly by the Client:

All influencer compensation and fees, if applicable
All performer, artist, celebrity, and talent fees
Venue rental, equipment hire, and production costs
Marketing materials, promotional items, and gifts
Permits, licenses, and regulatory fees
Any other third-party operational expenses

The Service Provider acts solely in a coordination capacity for all third-party engagements and is not a party to any financial arrangements between the Client and third parties.

5. Terms and Conditions

5.1 Engagement Period

The Engagement Period shall consist of consecutive days, with specific start and end dates to be mutually agreed upon in writing prior to commencement.

5.2 Validity Period

This Agreement remains valid for acceptance for seven (7) calendar days from the date of this Agreement. Pricing and resource availability are subject to change after this period.

5.3 Cancellation Policy

In the event of Client-initiated cancellation following execution of this Agreement, the initial 50% payment is non-refundable as it covers committed planning, coordination resources, and opportunity costs.

5.4 Scope Limitations

Services are limited to those explicitly outlined in Section 2 of this Agreement. Any additional requirements or services requested by the Client will be assessed and quoted separately as addenda to this Agreement.

5.5 Post-Engagement Services

Post-engagement advisory consultation services as described in Section 3 are available on an hourly basis at rates to be mutually agreed upon separately from this engagement.

5.6 Client Cooperation Obligations

The Client shall provide all information, materials, access, and cooperation reasonably requested by the Service Provider to facilitate delivery of the services. Failure to comply with such requests in a timely manner shall constitute a violation of this Agreement and may result in suspension or termination of services, without prejudice to any fees already due.
